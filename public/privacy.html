Privacy Policy — Chrono Empire

Effective date: 10 July 2026

Chrono Empire (“the Game”, “we”) is a single-player idle game. We designed it to need as little of your data as possible. This policy explains what is collected, why, and your choices.

1. Information we collect

  • Game progress — your save (businesses, currencies, settings) is stored locally on your device. It is only sent off-device if you tap “Back up now” in the Cloud Backup screen, in which case it is stored under a random, anonymous backup code.
  • Leaderboard — if you submit a score, the display name you type and your score number are stored so other players can see the ranking. Providing a name is optional.
  • Advertising identifiers — our ad partner (Google AdMob) may access your device’s advertising ID to show rewarded ads. On iOS this only happens if you allow tracking when prompted; otherwise you receive non-personalized ads.
  • Purchases — in-app purchases are processed by Apple, Google and RevenueCat. We receive a confirmation that a product was bought; we never see your card or payment details.

We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, contacts, photos or location.

2. How we use it

  • To save and restore your game progress.
  • To show the optional global leaderboard.
  • To show rewarded ads and process optional purchases that support development.

3. Third-party services

  • Google AdMob — advertising. Google Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — in-app purchase management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Google Firebase — optional cloud backup and leaderboard storage. Firebase Privacy

4. Children

The Game is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Where required, ads are served in a non-personalized manner.

5. Your choices & data deletion

  • You can wipe all local data any time via More → Delete save.
  • On iOS you can decline ad tracking in the system prompt or in Settings.
  • To delete a cloud backup or leaderboard entry associated with your backup code, contact us at the address below with that code and we will remove it.

6. Contact

Questions or deletion requests: 12hrsofficial@gmail.com

7. Changes
